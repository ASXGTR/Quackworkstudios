Privacy Policy

Duckmon Collectable Card Multiverse

Quackwork Studios respects your privacy. This game and website use several trusted third‑party services to provide authentication, gameplay features, account management, and secure hosting.

What Data We Collect

Duckmon Collectable Card Multiverse only collects the minimum information required to create and maintain a player account. This includes:

• Email address (used for login and verification)
• Username (chosen by the player)
• Supabase User ID (automatically generated)
• Gameplay data such as progress, inventory, or settings (if applicable)

Quackwork Studios does not collect passwords, payment information, device identifiers, or any unnecessary personal data.

How Your Data Is Used

Your information is used solely for:

• Creating and managing your game account
• Allowing you to log in securely
• Saving your game progress
• Sending verification or password‑reset emails
• Protecting the game from abuse or unauthorized access

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties for marketing.

Third‑Party Services

Duckmon uses trusted providers to operate securely:

• Supabase – authentication, account storage, and database
• Google – email delivery
• Unity – engine services and platform features
• Cloudflare – domain, DNS, and security protection

These services act as data processors on behalf of Quackwork Studios. Players should contact Quackwork Studios directly for any data requests. Third‑party providers cannot assist players directly.

Data Protection and Compliance

Quackwork Studios follows standard data protection principles, including transparency, security, minimal data collection, and user control. All data is processed in accordance with the Terms of Service and Privacy Policies of the providers listed above.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account remains active.

If you delete your account or request removal:

• Your account will be disabled immediately
• All associated data will be permanently deleted within 30 days
• Backups (if any) will be purged automatically during their normal rotation cycle

Data Removal Requests

Players may request account deletion or data removal at any time by contacting:

mail@quackworkstudios.com

We will verify the request and remove all associated data within the timeframe listed above.

Future Plans

Duckmon Collectable Card Multiverse is an evolving project, and future updates may introduce new features, platforms, or services. Planned future releases may include distribution through Steam and Itch.io.

These platforms may provide limited user information such as platform usernames, display names, or platform-specific user IDs (e.g., SteamID). Any information provided by these platforms will be used solely for gameplay features and account functionality.

All monetary transactions on Steam are handled entirely by Valve Corporation. Quackwork Studios does not receive or store any payment information. Demo versions distributed through Itch.io do not involve any financial transactions.

As new features or services are added, this Privacy Policy will be updated to reflect how player information is used, stored, and protected.
