About Me

Solo dev, dad, husband, building games one stubborn idea at a time.

Say hi

My name is John—solo game dev, dad, husband, and the creator of Quackwork Studios. What started as a hobby turned into a full creative obsession. I build in Unity, experiment with AI tools, and learn whatever I need to bring ideas to life.

Then came Duckmon. It evolved into a lore-heavy, multiverse-spanning 2D card battler inspired by Triple Triad. Ducks replaced zombies, and suddenly everything clicked. Duckmon is now the heart of Quackwork Studios.

I handle everything—art, systems, coding, audio, and design. I use AI tools as creative amplifiers; every mechanic and idea still comes through me. I am the director; the AI is just part of the toolkit.
